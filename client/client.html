Toronto's subways, streetcars, and bus routes combine to make the TTC the third largest transit system in North America.1

By tracing all possible trips originating at a particular origin point, this tool allows you to explore which areas of Toronto are best – and worst – served by transit.

Select some options and hit the play button to get started!
